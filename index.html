Why?

Daily Dose of Positivity was started because I am sick and tired of all the doom and gloom in the media today. The world is not ending. 2016 was not the worst year of all time. It was the best. 2017 will be even better. In fact, every year the world gets better and better. And not in some kumbaya, spiritual sense. Factually, 2016 was the best year ever to be a human being. Life expectancy is increasing, poverty rates are plummeting and famine is becoming a thing of the past.

Almost everybody thinks the world is getting worse. In fact, only 6% of Americans think the world is getting better. In France, that figure is 3%. Daily Dose of Positivity was started to show people that life really is good and getting better every year. Hopefully, by taking 30 seconds out of your day to read an inspiring fact, you can start to see the world for the incredible place that it is.
