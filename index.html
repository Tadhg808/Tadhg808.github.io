Why

The world is not ending. 2016 was not the worst year of all time. It was the best. 2017 will be even better. In fact, every year the world gets better and better. Life expectancy is increasing, poverty rates are plummeting and famine is becoming a thing of the past.

Almost everybody thinks the world is getting worse. In fact, only 6% of Americans think the world is getting better. Daily Dose of Positivity is here to help people understand that the world really is getting better. Hopefully, by taking 30 seconds out of your day, you can start to appreciate all of the incredible things about life today.
